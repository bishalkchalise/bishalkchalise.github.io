Hello! I am a third year PhD candidate at Deakin University.
My research examines how institutional structures and political incentives shape developmental outcomes. I focus on two main themes: first, the ways in which political incentives influence the provision and delivery of public services. For instance, my current work shows how local politicians affect post-earthquake reconstruction and how these influences can be constrained through effective program design. Second, I study how institutional design itself can generate unintended consequences, such as social conflict.
